About Us

Union of Astronomy, Bandaranayake College, founded on the 20th of March, 1996, has been a beacon of celestial exploration and scientific curiosity for nearly three decades. Registered at the Arthur C. Clarke Institute for Modern Technology in 1997, the union quickly established itself as a pioneering force in astronomical education. That same year, in December, the union held its first night sky observational camp—a modest beginning that would set the stage for a remarkable legacy. The Union of Astronomy at Bandaranayake College stands as a testament to the power of curiosity and the boundless possibilities that lie beyond our world. Its history is not just one of numbers, but of dreams realized, horizons expanded, and a collective gaze turned skyward in search of the unknown.
